Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

GhostLua ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Discord username and user ID when you choose to log in via Discord OAuth
  • Any messages or requests you send via our Discord server

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Standard server access logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps)
  • Download counts associated with Steam AppIDs (no personal identifiers)

1.3 Locally Stored Data

Download history, starred games, and session data are stored in your browser's localStorage only. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the GhostLua service
  • To identify your account if you choose to log in
  • To monitor download popularity and maintain our database
  • To respond to support requests submitted via Discord

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share aggregated, anonymized statistics (e.g., "top downloaded games") publicly. We use Steam's public APIs to retrieve game metadata — please see Steam's Privacy Policy for their data practices.

4. Data Retention

Server logs are retained for up to 30 days and then deleted. Discord login data is retained as long as your account is active or until you request deletion.

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of any personal data we hold about you by contacting us via our Discord server. Browser-stored data can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.

6. Cookies

GhostLua does not use cookies for tracking. We only use localStorage for functionality (history, favorites). No third-party advertising cookies are used.

7. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Join us on Discord.
