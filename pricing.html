Frequently Asked Questions

How do downloads reset?
Your download count resets on the 1st of every month. Unused downloads don't roll over.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! Cancel anytime and you'll keep access until the end of your billing period. No questions asked.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and cryptocurrency through our secure payment portal.
What happens if I hit my limit?
You'll see an upgrade prompt. You can upgrade instantly to continue downloading, or wait until your limit resets next month.
