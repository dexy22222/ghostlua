Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade?
Join our Discord server, head to the #upgrade channel, and follow the pinned instructions to receive a promo code. Paste it in the box above or click an upgrade button.
What are batch downloads?
Pro and Master users can select multiple games from search results and download all their lua files in a single zip — perfect for setting up a new PC or grabbing a whole series at once.
What is early access?
Master subscribers get access to newly added games before they're available to everyone else, plus a Master role in our Discord.
How do downloads reset?
Your download count resets on the 1st of every month. Unused downloads don't roll over.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — each code activates a plan for 30 days. Simply don't renew and you'll return to Free automatically when it expires.
What happens if I hit my download limit?
You'll see an upgrade prompt. Redeem a promo code to continue instantly, or wait until your limit resets on the 1st of next month.
